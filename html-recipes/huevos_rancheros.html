Huevos Rancheros

The Huevos rancheros are the ideal breakfast, since it contains distinctive ingredients of the mexican gastronomy

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Tomatoes
  • Chile serrano
  • Eggs
  • Refried beans
  • Tortillas

Preparation

Part one - Sauce

  • Wash the tomatoes, chiles and onions
  • Put them inside the blender
  • Pour the blend in a cooking pot
  • Simmer for 10-15 minutes, add salt to taste

    • Part two - Fry

    1. Fry the tortillas and then let them drain
    2. Prepare the crashed eggs in a pan
    3. Place two tortillas in the dish
    4. Place a egg on each tortilla
    5. Finally, pour sauce on the eggs
    6. Serve with the fried beans and slices of avocado
