Chilaquiles

The chilaquiles are a delicious saucer from Mexico, is common eat it at the breakfast with eggs, bread and a cup of coffe.

    Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cooking oil
  • Chile serrano
  • Tomatillo
  • Onion
  • Cream
  • Panela cheese
  • 2 Garlic clove

    Preparation

    First part - Totopos

  1. Take the tortillas and cut them in 8 equal slices.
  2. Fry the tortillas.
  3. Remove excess oil, letting the tortillas drain.

    Second part - Sauce

  1. Peel the tomatillos.
  2. Wash tomatillos, onion and chiles.
  3. Add everything to the blender with the garlics and a cup of water.
  4. Put a cooking pot in the stove with a bit of oil and onion.
  5. Empty the blend in the cooking pot, add salt and simmer for 10-15 minutes.

    Serve

  1. Put totopos in a dish, add a good amount of sauce.
  2. Complete with cream and slices of panela cheese to taste.
