Guacamole

The guacamole is the most consumed mexican appetizer in the US

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Chile serrano
  • Onion
  • Tomatoe
  • Lemon
  • Totopos

Preparation

  1. Cut the avocado in half and remove the pulp with a spoon
  2. Dice the tomatoes, onions and chiles
  3. Place the dices and the pulp in a molcajete
  4. Squeeze lemon and grind to puree
  5. Add salt to taste, accompany eith totopos
